Why Adopt a Street Dog?

Street dogs are often misunderstood and overlooked, but they make wonderful pets with a little love and care.

Save a Life: By adopting a street dog, you're literally saving a life and giving a second chance to a deserving animal.

Unique Personality: Street dogs often have unique personalities shaped by their experiences, making them loyal and loving companions.

Gratitude: Street dogs are often extremely grateful for a second chance and will shower you with love and affection.

Low Maintenance: Many street dogs are already house-trained and may even know basic commands, making the transition to your home easier.

My dog is acting weird, is it normal?

Dogs have many reasons for acting out of character. Try to remember if there has been a change in routine, diet, or environment. If you're still unsure, consult with a vet.
